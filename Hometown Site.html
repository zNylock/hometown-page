Top Three Activities to do in Toronto

Explore Kensington Market

A vibrant, multicultural neighborhood with unique shops, international cuisine, and street art that captures Toronto's diverse spirit.

Catch an event at Scotiabank Arena

Experience the electric atmosphere as Toronto's passionate fans cheer on the Maple Leafs (NHL) or Raptors (NBA).

Enjoy a show at the Queen Elizabeth Theatre

Catch top musical acts and renowned comedians in this intimate, renovated venue that delivers excellent acoustics and sightlines.
